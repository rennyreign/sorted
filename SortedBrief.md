# Project Name: Sorted

# AGENT BRIEF — “SORTED” (Working Name)

## Objective

Create a fast, simple website that converts UK small business owners who need online work done properly — without agency prices.

This is a **modern digital production service**, not a traditional agency.

The site must make users feel:

> “I can finally get something decent done quickly, without overpaying or dealing with hassle.”
> 

---

## Core Truth

Good online work doesn’t need to be expensive anymore.

---

## Positioning

We use a faster, smarter production model to deliver clean, professional digital work at small-business-friendly prices.

Do not mention AI.

Do not explain how it works.

Only show the outcome.

---

## Primary Offer

Users can request:

- Simple websites (1-page or small builds)
- Landing pages
- Logo & brand design
- Flyers, menus, social posts
- Google Business Profile setup/fix
- TikTok ads setup
- Facebook ads setup
- Instagram ads setup
- AI live chat agents for websites
- Website edits / fixes
- General “I need this sorted” requests

Always include:

> Or just tell us what you need.
> 

---

## Target User

- UK small business owner
- Time-poor
- Cost-conscious
- Not technical
- Wants something that looks good (not cheap-looking)
- Doesn’t want calls, jargon, or upselling
- Prefers async, with optional human support

---

## Tone & Language

- Plain UK English
- Direct, human, grounded
- Slightly sharp, never clever

Avoid:

- “solutions”
- “growth”
- “premium”
- “world-class”
- “cutting-edge”

Use:

- “done properly”
- “clean and modern”
- “priced clearly”
- “no messing about”

## Core Mechanic (CRITICAL)

### Intake Agent (Primary Conversion)

This is the main feature.

Must feel:

- Fast
- Effortless
- Low pressure
- Outcome-driven

---

### Intake Flow

Ask:

1. What do you need?
2. What’s your business?
3. Do you have anything already? (optional upload)
4. When do you need it?

Then confirm:

> Thanks — we’ll review this and send a price shortly.
> 
> 
> Once approved, you’ll have something to review within 24–48 hours. Rarely longer.
> 

---

### Optional Human Path

Secondary option only:

> Prefer to speak to someone?
> 

Options:

- Request a call
- WhatsApp / chat

Do not push this.

---

## Turnaround Expectation (CORE TO CONVERSION)

- Users should expect to see initial work within 24–48 hours after approval
- It should be clearly stated: “Rarely longer”
- Always frame as “something to review”, not final delivery
- This message must appear in:
    - Hero
    - Process
    - Intake confirmation

Speed is part of the product — not a bonus.
